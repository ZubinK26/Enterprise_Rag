# Enterprise Customer Support Policy

Our support organization delivers consistent transparent responses measuring reliability protecting revenue honoring contractual service level agreements enabling customers trusting operational partnerships during incidents configuration changes migrations expansions.

## Ticket intake & ownership

Primary tracking happens inside the approved service management product ensuring history durability auditability integration with engineering escalations finance credits renewals legal holds. Agents must avoid shadow spreadsheets duplicating state diverging from canonical records executives sample quality monthly.

Duplicate tickets merge carefully preserving customer-visible narratives linking related defect clusters preventing fragmented war rooms during widespread outages confusing communications.

Agents capture environment metadata versions subscription tiers impacted user counts replication strategies encryption modes hosting regions helping engineers reproduce failures faster shortening windows customers endure pain.

Agents sanitize attachments removing secrets tokens before uploads routing through secure ingestion channels respecting privacy obligations contractual data residency promises.

Agents acknowledge receipt promptly even before root-cause clarity because silence erodes trust especially during revenue-impacting degradations.

## Priority definitions & response expectations

Priority one incidents represent complete production outages pervasive authentication failures ransomware activations uncontained data exposures requiring immediate paging bridges leadership notifications customer statements coordinated carefully avoiding contradictory promises.

Acknowledgments for priority one must publish within fifteen minutes during published coverage rotations holidays included through follow-the-sun handoffs documented shift boards visible organization-wide transparency preventing confusion about who wakes next.

Priority two incidents represent materially degraded throughput elevated error ratios partial feature loss significant customer-visible latency absent total blackout warranting hourly updates until stabilized or downgrade criteria satisfied through metric reviews.

Acknowledgments for priority two publish within sixty minutes unless correlated incident umbrellas already summarized publicly reducing spam customers appreciate consolidated banners referencing authoritative dashboards.

Priority three covers standard break-fix issues configuration guidance onboarding assistance non-critical bugs affecting limited cohorts acknowledged within four business hours escalated when reproduction solidifies.

Priority four tracks improvement ideas documentation gaps training requests acknowledged within two business days slotted into capacity planning without binding delivery dates unless product management commits roadmaps.

Severity inflation wastes engineering capacity damages credibility when true catastrophes strike therefore duty managers arbitrate disputes using impact matrices referencing dollars at risk customer counts contractual breach exposure regulatory reporting triggers.

## Escalation & communication discipline

Escalations package reproduction steps hypotheses tested mitigations suggested log excerpts redacted timelines summarizing customer quotes engineering bridges require agendas note-takers action owners preventing circular debates frustrating tired responders at three a.m.

War rooms assign separate internal versus external communicators synchronizing statements customers hear consistent restoration estimates confidence bands caveats unknowns investigation threads parallel legal reviews when contractual penalties imminent.

Post-incident reviews document contributing factors preventive actions tracking owners deadlines leadership visibility closing loops transparently rebuilding trust after painful events.

Support engineers must never promise permanent root-cause conclusions before evidence matures preferring language acknowledging ongoing investigation alongside concrete interim workarounds customers execute safely.

Support engineers rotate on-call fairly documenting handoffs highlighting lingering risks fragile mitigations customer sensitivities personality nuances influencing communication tone success.

Support engineers honor knowledge base maintenance obligations updating articles after resolved incidents preventing future teams rediscovering solved mysteries wasting hours customers pay indirectly through delays.

Violations like sharing credentials publicly ignoring secure transfer workflows downloading entire production datasets onto personal devices trigger security reviews access revocations retraining pathways restoring disciplined collaboration cultures customers expect.
